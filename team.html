Meet the Team

TestDrive is developed by the Cornell Social Media Lab, with generous support from the Program for Research on Youth Development and Engagement (PRYDE), Cornell's Bronfenbrenner Center for Translational Research (BCTR), and the National Science Foundation.

People

Program Director: Dr. Natalie Bazarova

Dr. Bazarova is an Associate Professor in the Department of Communication at Cornell University. As Director of the Social Media Lab, she studies how people communicate and relate to one another in social media. Her recent research focuses on online self-disclosure and privacy, with some of her new papers examining disclosure motivations, interpretations, language of disclosure, and its social and psychological effects.

Program Advisor: Dr. Janis Whitlock

Dr. Whitlock is a Research Scientist at Cornell's Bronfenbrenner Center for Translational Research, and the Director of The Cornell Research Program on Self-Injury and Recovery. She is interested in the social, cultural, and contextual factors that influence adolescent development and identity formation.

Technical Director: Dr. Dominic DiFranzo

Dr. DiFranzo is a postdoctoral associate in the Social Media Lab. He specializes in creating research-based technological interventions to social issues. He oversees the technical development and direction for the TestDrive platform.

Project Manager: Jessie Taft

Jessie is the Lab Manager for the Social Media Lab, and coordinates curriculum/content development and outreach for Social Media TestDrive.
