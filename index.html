Learn digital citizenship skills through a social media simulation

Social Media TestDrive lets students practice digital citizenship skills within a safe and protected platform.

Upstanding to Cyberbullying

This module will teach students how to detect signs of cyberbullying and how to respond to cyberbullying situations. Students will be able to practice "upstanding" behaviors to cyberbullying such as supporting the target, confronting the bully, and reporting the cyberbullying posts to the social media site.

Is it Private Information?

In this module, students will learn about what kinds of information are safe to share with different social media audiences and identify examples of private information that is inappropriate to share on social media.
