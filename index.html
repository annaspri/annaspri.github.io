Practice digital literacy through a social media simulation.

Social Media TestDrive lets young people practice digital citizenship skills in a simulated, realistic social media environment.

Our Vision

We hope to provide a kind, safe social media environment for kids to connect and engage.

Safety first

TestDrive is a simulation, so kids can learn online behavior without real-life consequences.

Essential topics

Lessons cover a broad range of skills for digital literacy and online behavior relevant to today's kids.

100% free

TestDrive is a free, constantly-updated resource for educators to incorporate into their classrooms and cirriculum.

Education for the digital age

TestDrive simulates realistic digital dilemmas and scenarios that young people may encounter as they enter the social media world.

Each lesson teaches essential digital citizenship concepts, covering topics such as privacy, online identities, cyberbullying, and more.

Prepare kids for the online world

TestDrive is targeted for middle school-aged youth (ages 9–13) who are likely enter into the social media world in the near future.

TestDrive lessons can be done individually, in pairs, or as a guided group activity in educational settings to facilitate learning outcomes.

A trusted resource for the classroom

Educators can use TestDrive to engage with students and spark conversations around what it means to be prosocial and ethical citizens online.

TestDrive’s lessons are aligned with Common Sense Education to provide a curriculum that covers emerging topics in digital literacy.

Great for parents, too

TestDrive lessons can be completed from the comfort of your own home, and we encourage parents to try it out with their children.

We do not store or collect any personal or identifying information, so your child’s data will always be safe on our platform.
