Lessons

Upstanding to Cyberbullying

This module will teach students how to detect signs of cyberbullying and how to respond to cyberbullying situations. Students will be able to practice "upstanding" behaviors to cyberbullying such as supporting the target, confronting the bully, and reporting the cyberbullying posts to the social media site.

Aligned Common Sense Lesson: Upstanders and Allies: Taking Action Against Cyberbullying

News on Social Media

Evaluating the quality of news articles on social media is a vital skill for today’s youth. This module will teach students about why fake news exists and how to identify the telltale signs of fake news on social media.

Aligned Common Sense Lesson: Finding Credible News

Is it Private Information?

In this module, students will learn about what kinds of information are safe to share with different social media audiences and identify examples of private information that is inappropriate to share on social media.

Aligned Common Sense Lesson: Chatting Safely Online

Online Identities

This module will help students manage how they present themselves on social media. Students will learn about the different audiences that might see what they post online, and think about the things they share and the way they present themselves to others.

Aligned Common Sense Lesson: Who Are You Online?

Taking Care of Your Digital Footprint

In this module, students will learn about how actions on social media create a digital footprint. They will learn skills to positively shape their own digital footprintsas well as how to be responsible for other people’s digital footprints.

Aligned Common Sense Lesson: Social Media and Digital Footprints

Social Media Privacy

In this module, students will learn how social media sites collect information about users as well as strategies for protecting their privacy on social media. They will be able to practice managing their privacy settings page and practice analyzing a Privacy Policy.

Aligned Common Sense Lesson: Being Aware of What You Share
